## 2. American Daily Rules
1. Debit expenses and losses.
2. Credit revenues and gains.
3. Debit assets.
4. Credit liabilities.
5. Debit drawings.
6. Credit capital.
7. Debit is on the left; Credit is on the right.
8. Assets & Expenses increase with Debit.
9. Liabilities, Equity & Revenues increase with Credit.
